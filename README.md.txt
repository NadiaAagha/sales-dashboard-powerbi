# Sales Dashboard (2022–2024)

This Power BI dashboard provides an analytical overview of company sales between 2022 and 2024.  
It visualizes key metrics like total sales, cost, quantity, and brand performance.

---

##  Problem Statement

The company’s sales data was stored in multiple files, making it difficult to identify top-performing categories, track costs, and analyze regional sales trends.  
To solve this, an interactive Power BI dashboard was created to consolidate data and generate visual insights.

---

##  Insights / Findings

1. **Monitors** and **Mobiles** were the highest-selling categories (290 and 236 units respectively).  
2. **Intel** led in brand sales (18.04%), followed by **Asus (16.6%)** and **MSI (13.71%)**.  
3. Sales were concentrated in **southern and western regions**.  
4. **Total Sales:** 16M, **Total Cost:** 12M → Profit: **4M**.  
5. Total **797 transactions** completed with **1994 total units** sold.  
6. **Printers** and **CPU Coolers** had the lowest sales, suggesting marketing or inventory review.
